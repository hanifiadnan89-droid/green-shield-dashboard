# Objection Handling

Use Connect → Why → Close.

## I Need to Think About It
"Completely understandable. Most people do need to think about it first. Which part of the process is still giving you some trouble?"

## I Need to Talk to My Spouse
"Yeah, I totally hear you. I'd probably do the same thing. Do you think they would have any questions I can help answer? What do you think their biggest concern would be?"

Then offer tentative close if appropriate (see message_templates.md).

## I Only Want One Treatment
"Yeah, I totally get it. One treatment can definitely get some results. The reason we recommend the year is that no product penetrates pest eggs, so one-time treatments often turn into customers calling back later. By coming every three months, we keep product active long enough to break the life cycle."

Close again.

## I Do Not Need Winter Service
"Pests do not just die in winter. Many overwinter and rebuild until spring. Keeping protection active through colder months helps reduce spring and summer activity."

## I Do Not Want Recurring Service
"The good news is it is just the minimum number of visits to make a lasting difference. If we can stick with it for the year, we can actually solve the issue instead of putting a temporary band-aid on it."

## Too Expensive
"I get it. Prices are high everywhere. The main reason people choose us is they get their money's worth. We are local, thorough, and our five-star reviews speak for themselves."

## Competitor Price Match
Only with approval. Green Pest Defense is often used as the low-cost competitor reference around $375 initial and $50/month for a 13-month agreement. Do not offer this unless authorized.

## Penalty to Cancel
"No major penalty. Since we give a discount on the first visit for customers who do the full year, all we ask is that if you stop before the fourth visit, you reimburse that initial discount, which is $150."
Canonical rule: see account_management_rules.md.

## Monthly Charge Objection
"It is the same total price, just broken into smaller monthly payments so it is easier."

## Pay by Check
"You can. Most people prefer card because they do not need to be home every visit or mail a check every time."

## Credit Card Trust
"We use a PCI-compliant vault. After I enter the card and save it, I cannot even read it back."

## Safety Concerns
Products are family and pet friendly once dry. Canonical safety wording: see services_and_pricing.md.

## Unhappy Customer Wants to Cancel
Do not immediately cancel. Apologize and offer to send someone out to make it right.

## Happy Customer Wants to Cancel
Explain benefits of staying: no new upfront cost, no price increase, no contract going forward after agreement term.

## Downgrade
Offer a maintenance plan if appropriate. Minimums: IQ $39/month or $195/service. RIT/BIT $49/month or $225/service.
