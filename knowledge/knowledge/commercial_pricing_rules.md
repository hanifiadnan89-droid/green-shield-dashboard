# Commercial Pricing Rules

Commercial accounts are any buildings that are not a single-family home. This includes duplexes, multi-unit rentals, apartments, businesses, restaurants, offices, warehouses, group homes, Airbnbs, and landlord-managed rentals with multiple units.

Even a single-family home with multiple units is considered commercial.

For residential (single-family) pricing, see services_and_pricing.md.

---

## Commercial Account Identification

Examples of commercial accounts:
- Duplex
- Multi-unit apartment building
- Rental building with multiple units
- Business, restaurant, office, warehouse
- Condo association
- Church, group home
- Airbnb or landlord-managed rental with multiple units

When unsure whether a property is residential or commercial, ask:
"Is this a single-family home, a rental property with multiple units, or a business?"

## Exterior Only Rodent Baiting

For accounts that want exterior rodent bait stations only — no interior treatment.

### Pricing Formula
- Equipment (initial): $35 per station
- Monthly service: $99 base for up to 3 stations, plus $4 per additional station per month
- Minimum: 3 stations

### Station Count Guideline
Use roughly the number of units minus a small number:
- 6-unit building → 4 stations
- 8-unit building → 6 stations
- 10-unit building → 8 stations

### Frequency
- Monthly is recommended
- Bi-monthly available: same initial price, monthly fee discounted 25%

### Worked Example
A 10-unit building with 8 stations:
- Initial: (8 × $35) + first month fee = $280 + $111 = $391
- Monthly: ~$111/month
- Bi-monthly alternative: $391 initial, ~$81/month

### Red Note for Technician
"PESTS: Rodents only. AREAS: Exterior only. Move quickly only rebaiting exterior stations. No need to check in with anyone."

## AI Behavior for Commercial Quotes

Keep commercial replies simple. Do not dump the full pricing table unless the customer asks for it. Ask the missing qualifier first, then give the relevant number.

**Never quote from commercial tables without the required qualifier:**
- Multi-unit: must know unit count first
- Business: must know approximate square footage first
- Exterior rodent baiting: must know station count or unit count first

**One question at a time:**

| Situation | Ask |
|---|---|
| Multi-unit building, unit count unknown | "How many units are in the building?" |
| Business, sq footage unknown | "Do you know the approximate square footage of the building?" |
| Exterior rodent baiting, scope unknown | "Do you know about how many exterior bait stations you may need, or how many units or buildings we would be protecting?" |
| Property type unclear | "Is this a single-family home, a rental property with multiple units, or a business?" |

**Flag for human review:**
- 10,000+ sq ft commercial properties (manager approval required)
- Complex quotes involving multiple buildings
- Unusual pest combinations on a commercial account
- Commercial customer asking for same-day or next-day service
