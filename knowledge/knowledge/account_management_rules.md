# Account Management Rules

## Notes
After every interaction, add clear notes. Yellow note example:
“SALES: 1/15/2026 - No answer, left voicemail and texted - SRC”

## Red notes
Use for commercial, custom, special deals, special tech instructions. Must be visible to tech.
Format:
“PESTS: rodents and common bugs. AREAS: interior and exterior of duplex. Customer wants pictures for exclusion work.”

## Owner not present
Try to have owner meet tech. If not possible, send someone else or provide home/meter photos. Welcome Letter must be signed online before service.

## Landlord/tenant
If tenant pays, written landlord permission is required before treating. Upload authorization.

## Commercial
Business/organization or landlord-managed rental setup usually counts as commercial.

## Multiple properties
Each unique address needs its own account. Link through Properties tab.

## Change of owner
Create new account for new owner. Do not overwrite old customer due privacy risk. Copy important service notes only.

## Upgrades
Create new agreement/subscription, schedule initial, freeze old subscription if replacing, notify sales manager to avoid double billing/commission issues.

## Technician sales
If technician helped sell upgrade/bundle, tech bonus usually applies. Confirm details and pricing with customer.

## Cancellation Save Flow
Try to save first. Offer reservice. For happy canceling customers, explain no new upfront cost, grandfathered pricing, and no contract going forward.

**$150 Cancellation Reimbursement (canonical source — all other files reference this):**
If a customer cancels before completing four visits, they reimburse the $150 initial discount that was applied at signup. This applies to IQ, RIT, and BIT. It does not apply to TMM.
