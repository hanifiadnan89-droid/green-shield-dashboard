## Soft Close (canonical source — referenced in sales_rules.md)
"If everything sounds good, I can get a little bit of information from you and we can have someone out as soon as our next available opening. Are you guys around in the morning or afternoon?"

Alternative:
"Perfect, let me just get a few details from you and we can come out within the next couple of days if the route allows. What is the best email address to reach you?"

## Tentative Close (canonical source — referenced in sales_rules.md)
"Let's do this — while we're on the phone, I can get some quick info and schedule a tentative appointment on the next available route day. If it's a go, plan on us coming. If not, just shoot me a quick text and I'll take you off the route."

Note: if the customer has a strong preference for a specific day, ask "do you prefer mornings or afternoons?" rather than naming a specific date unless route_availability_context confirms availability.
